Frequently Added Questions

When will my item(s) ship?

Orders take three days to process. We will ship your item on day four as well as forward you your tracking information.

What are your refund policies?

We offer free shipping for our returns placed within 14 days of receipt and the full cost of your item will be returned via the form of payment. Returns after 14 days are subject to a shipping fee and store credit only.

What if I want to exchange an item instead?

We do not offer exchanges. We recommend that you purchase your replacement separately and send return the item you are unsatisfied with.
